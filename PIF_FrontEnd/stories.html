“


I was at my favorite Taco shop today in the Ithaca Commons. When I got to the checkout I paid and saw a tablet that asked if I’d like to donate a meal by paying for it now so a homeless person could it later. I just about teared up on the spot. I pressed a button to donate a taco and I even got to leave a comment for the person. When I went home and ate, the tacos tasted better than ever because I knew my donation would feed someone that night. To the person who needed it, I hope you enjoyed it as much as I did! - Gloria ”
